About

Mr. Preciado has been working in politics on the local and national level for over 10 years.He brings a wealth of professional experience to his work coming from both the public and private sector.

Before PolicyEdge, Sergio served as a political consultant for one of the top Public Affair firms in Los Angeles, with clients at the highest levels of the business, non-profit, trade association and political worlds. Preceding that, he was a Congressional staff member in Washington D.C., for a member of the U.S House of representatives. He is a resident of Los Angeles and spends his time in his community.

As the founder, Sergio is committed to giving new insight into local government.
